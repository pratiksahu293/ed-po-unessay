Key Concepts Through My Research

Model Minority Myth

My research shows that this myth pressures Asian youth to appear “perfect” while hiding inequality. Youth challenge this narrative online by exposing real struggles.

Microaggressions

Small comments deeply affect youth identity. Online communities now give youth language to name harm and find support.

Third Space

Digital spaces function as “third spaces” where youth safely explore race, culture, and power outside school and home.

Digital Activism

Youth organize protests, circulate counter-stories, and mobilize peers through hashtags, videos, and digital storytelling.

Critical Media Literacy

Youth analyze and challenge harmful portrayals instead of passively consuming media.
